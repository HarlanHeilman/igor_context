World Wide Web 
You will find our Web site at:
http://www.wavemetrics.com/
You can also choose HelpWaveMetrics Home Page.
Our Web site contains a page for searching our support database, and links to Igor-related FTP sites and to 
Igor users’ Web pages. In addition, it contains a number of cool graphs. We are always grateful for new cool 
graphs. Contact us at sales@wavemetrics.com if you have a cool graph to share.
